As we need some permits to continue the route that we want to do, this morning we need to prepare some paperwork here in Kaza. It's fine because today we'll visit places near here and we have plenty of time. Again, Ti√† feels the altitude sickness. It seems that his body is not reacting well sleeping at more than 3500 m above the sea level. 

After the breakfast and the paperwork we drive to Langza, which is situated at an altitude of 4400 m and is divided into two sections, Langza Yongma (lower) and Langza Gongma (upper). It houses a population of approximately 137 people and an around 1000 years old golden Budha statue.
