Day 185

Finally! Today we'll dive. One of the dives is a bit dissapointing because the coral is all dead... it seems that the earthquake affected badly this region; It's something heartbreaking to see. In the other hand I get to see my first shark (a blacktip shark which was resting in a cave), Ti√† was not lucky enough. But we also have seen some Hawksville turtles, lots of fishes as usual and... a pigmy seahorse! Amazing!!!!

It's nice to feel how confident we get as more as we dive... we love it!

As I left the phone at the hotel to dive, there aren't pics of it... but at the afternoon we have a nice walk around the little village.
